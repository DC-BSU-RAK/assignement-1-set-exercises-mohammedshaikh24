God of War - Plot Overview

God of War is an action-adventure game that follows Kratos, a warrior who once took revenge on the Greek gods. After destroying the Greek world, he finds himself in Norse mythology. The game begins with the death of his wife, Faye. Kratos and his son Atreus go on a journey to scatter her ashes from the highest peak in the Nine Realms. Along the way, they face gods, monsters, and shocking truths about Atreus and Kratos' past.
