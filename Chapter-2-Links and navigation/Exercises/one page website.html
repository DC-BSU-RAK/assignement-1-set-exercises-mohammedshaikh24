God of War - Plot Overview

God of War is an action-adventure game which has the main character called Kratos. In the previous games he had seeked revenge for the death of his family and killed all the greek gods and destroyed the whole universe and this game is the continuation, in which he ends up in norse methodology. Faye, His wife dies and Kratos and his son go on a journey. The game mainly looks at their journey to spread the ashes of Kratos' wife(Faye) in the highest peak of the Nine Realms which is the Giants' Realm, while facing gods, monsters, and uncovering secrets of the past that even shocked Kratos as the story progressed.

Key Characters

  • Kratos: The Greek god of war, who now lives in the Norse place with his son, Atreus and helps him survive the world full of evil and leave his past behind.
  • Atreus: Kratos' young son, who is one of the main characters to the story which plays a big role and has a mysterious connection to the gods as story progress we learn more about him.
  • Freya: The Norse goddess who is an ally, but becomes a major issue in the story because every character villain or main character are all connected.
